# Feature List

## Public Website
- **Homepage:** Sliders, Govt Website links, Benefits, FAQ.
- **Content:** About Us, Privacy Policy (`PolicyPageFrontend`).
- **Enquiry:** General contact, Corporate enquiry.
- **Registration:** Student and Corporate registration.

## Student Module
- **Authentication:** Signup, Login, OTP Verification, Password Reset.
- **Dashboard:** Status overview.
- **Application Flow:**
    - Basic Details (`studentform`)
    - Qualifications (`addQualifications`)
    - Additional Details (`additionalDetailStore`)
    - Photo Upload
    - Final Submit
- **Payment:** Razorpay integration, Coupon codes.
- **Post-Payment:** Admit Card download, Result download.
- **Scholarship:** Claim form, Status check.

## Corporate/Institute Module
- **Registration & Enquiry:** Separate flows.
- **Coupons:** Request and management.

## Center Management
- **Lists:** View centers.
- **Management:** Add/Edit centers.

## Administration
- **Dashboard:** Redirects to `/administrator`.
